About us

I'm a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

I've made my home base in Providence, Rhode Island with my small, growing family. My journey into tech started with a degree in journalism. As I started my writing online, I was fascinated with how easy I could get my voice out there. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.

That curiosity then opend a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realised I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
